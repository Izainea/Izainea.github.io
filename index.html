ABOUT ME

Hello, I am an applied mathematician from the National University of Colombia who has worked as a teacher at various private universities in Bogota. I am passionate about good problems and I am a Python lover, on this page you will find some of the tutorials and repositories that I use in my classes.

If you are a student who came to this site looking for information or help for a course, I invite you to check the links to the different projects that we have developed in some classes. Check my contact information if you need me.
